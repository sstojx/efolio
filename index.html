Proceed

Summary

The following document aims to justify that I have met the Australian Professional Teaching Standards set by the Australian Institute for Teaching and School Leadership through evidences gathered from my studies at the Christian Heritage College (CHC), the two practicum placements and my employment at Warrigal Rd State School. I have done this by addressing one sub-descriptor of one standard at a time providing evidences and giving brief justifications.  Documents used for multiple descriptors have been colour coded. Many standards descriptors have been addressed through the units of study I undertook during my education and training at the CHC.

06/05/2019

1. Professional Knowledge

1. Know students and how they learn

1.1 Physical, social and intellectual development and characteristics of student

Description

Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of physical, social and intellectual development and characteristics of students and how these may affect learning.

Evidence

Case study report, photographs, tasks list and academic literature. Photographs show helping students use concrete materials to complete tasks and 'Set Tasks' sheet prepared daily for a student with ASD. The Executive Function literature was used to help determine better ways of ensuring best egagement with tasks for the ASD diagnosed student in order to maximise his classroom learning. Selected suggestions are alternative note taking and alternate tasks.

Justification

The corresponding section of the case study shows how I have worked with the student and collaborated with the colleagues to determine the issues that affect one particular student’s learning. Photos show working with students of various levels of ability.
